Peaches & Cream Oatmeal

Breakfast Recipe

With this oatmeal recipe, you will be fully satisfied! Mix-ins of sweet peaches and rich pecans will make this one of your most memorable breakfasts.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups old fashioned rolled oats
  • ⅓ cup pecans chopped very small
  • 2 large peaches chopped small, about 2 cups
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3½ cups water
  • Milk, half and half, or cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine the oats, peaches, pecans, salt, and cinnamon in a medium-size saucepan.
  2. Add the water and stir to combine. Bring to a boil and immediately reduce to a simmer.
  3. Simmer for 4-5 minutes, stirring often.
  4. When the oats are tender, remove from the heat.
  5. Portion into serving bowls and add the toppings of your choice.
